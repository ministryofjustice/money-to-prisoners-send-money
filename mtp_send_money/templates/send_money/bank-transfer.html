3. The money is paid into the prisoner’s account

The money should arrive in the prisoner’s account in 1 working day. However, your bank may take longer to process payment (up to 3 working days).

No money is processed at weekends.

If you have any questions about how to make a payment, contact your bank or building society.

Remember, you can only use this service to send money to prisoners in HMP Bullingdon, HMP Brixton or HMP High Down.

You can’t make a money transfer in person from a bank.
